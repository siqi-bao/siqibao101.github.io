Bio

  • Dr. Siqi Bao is a senior R&D engineer at Baidu NLP, where she leads the foundation research of PLATO. She received her B.E. from Zhejiang University in 2012, and her Ph.D. from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2017.

  • Her research interests focus on dialogue system, pre-trained models, natural language processing, and medical image analysis.
